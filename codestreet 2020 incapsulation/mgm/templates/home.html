What is In-Capsulation?

The idea is to meet the demand of the people while bridging the gap of shortage as well as avoiding excess of supplies. Forecast fluctuations and divert resources accordingly. Inventory management is the first of Capital Management after all.

Why In-Capsulation?

We strive for satisfaction. Small and medium scale businesses are the next focus for revolutionizing the supply chain. This interface is for anticipating their needs. Look at our key features to know more.

Forecast Sales


Inventory is all about the numbers. Forecast upcoming sale numbers based on Sales history to meet market demand.

Manage stock


Predictions of possible fluctuations ensure that the supply doesn't end up as a deficit or a surplus. Meet market demand with accuracy and as well as sufficiency.

Allot resources


The specificity from these predictions will hinder wastage and allow businesses to divert the resources into specific products, which will be available for purchase rather than average the whole inventory line
